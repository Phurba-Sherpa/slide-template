Executive summary: Top 3 Narratives & Strategic Risk

Youth perceptions of manufacturing careers are dominated by financial anxiety, educational stigma, and work-life balance concerns-high-impact narratives requiring targeted intervention.

Critical Narrative Cluster

Financial Inadequacy Concerns

Manufacturing salaries perceived as insufficient for Singapore's high cost of living. Most prevalent narrative (17.5%) with high emotional intensity.

Skills Mismatch & Educational Stigma

Manufacturing viewed as career for those who "cannot make it" academically. Strong family/peer influence on perception (17.5%).

Work-Life Balance Fears

Concerns about long hours, shift work, and limited personal life.Particularly strong among university students (5.6%).
